## Implications for 10XAI
1. **They're $15/mo SELF-SERVE for the LOWER end.** 10XAI's "no-website flywheel" must NOT compete on price/DIY — it must compete on **proof + outbound**. Hocoos is "build it yourself in 5 min." 10XAI is "we built it for you, here's the live preview, here's why it'll convert your reviewers into customers."

3. **GBP integration is the wedge.** Hocoos has Local SEO + Google Business Profile integration as a *manual* setup. 10XAI's pitch: "your GBP IS the input — we generated this site from your reviews and photos."
4. **No agency program here either.** A consistent pattern across the SMB-builder category. Whitespace.
