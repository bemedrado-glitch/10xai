# AiSDR

> **The most replicable playbook for 10XAI.** Captured 2026-04-30.
> URL: https://aisdr.com/

> ★ This is the closest "best practice" benchmark to what 10XAI should build, especially around productized SDR + content depth + transparent pricing.

## Tagline
*"Send deeply researched, high-converting sales outreach"*

## ICP
Startups → mid-market. SaaS, Healthcare, E-commerce, Finance, EdTech, Services. US-centric (SF HQ).

## Products
| Product | Function |
|---|---|
| **AiSDR (platform)** | End-to-end AI SDR |
| **Enigma** | The branded AI SDR persona |
| **AI Strategist** | 20-min conversational tool that returns 5 GTM campaign ideas from a website URL |
| **Live AI Search** | Plain-English prospect search; daily-fresh lists |
| **AI Prospecting Tool** | Signal-triggered targeting |

## Pricing — TRANSPARENT
- **$900/mo** standard, billed quarterly
- Unlimited seats
- Credits-based (messages + lead search)
- Cancel anytime
- ROI claim: pays for itself in 2–3 closed deals; positive ROI in 30–60 days

## Differentials
- **Intent-first targeting** (signals, not databases)
- Deep research per message (LinkedIn posts, news, industry data)
- **Strategy before execution** — AI Strategist generates campaigns, not just templates
- Multi-channel: email + LinkedIn + phone + video + voice notes
- "Meetings, not activity" — 1–3 qualified mtgs per 100 leads, vs. <1% industry
- **Speed:** live in <1 week, first meetings in 3–8 days, 5.7–19% reply rates
- **Stack consolidation** — replaces Apollo + ZoomInfo + Clearbit + Clay + Lemlist + Instantly + ZeroBounce + Copy.ai

## Sales motion
**Strong inbound + light PLG.** Free AI Strategist tool, free GTM ideas, ROI calc, free Inc 5000 prospecting DB. Self-qualifies prospects before demo. Demo still gates final purchase.

## Funding & traction
- "Trusted by 250+ companies"
- "12,000 meetings booked for clients in 2025"
- Named wins: Novartis, Pfizer, Viatris, Disney, Airbnb, Delta, Salesforce, JPMorgan, Travelers, Nasdaq, Microsoft (mostly via case studies, likely small departmental wins)
- No funding/ARR disclosed

## Strengths
- **Quantified ROI everywhere** ($900 → 2–3 deals; 5.7–19% reply rate)
- **Density of proof** (10+ case studies + 250 logos + outcomes)
- **Honest "who AiSDR isn't for"** disqualification section
- **Eats own dog food** ("Speak with our AI" widget)
- **Transparent pricing** — rare in this space; major buyer-trust win
- **Stack-consolidation pitch** is the strongest economic story in the category

## Weaknesses (gaps 10XAI can exploit)
- **English-only / US-centric** — wide-open lane in PT-BR / ES
- **No freemium product** (free tools yes; free product no)
- **Single ICP angle** — broad, not vertical-specific
- **Limited integration breadth shown** vs. Relevance AI's 1,000+
- **No public AI-quality / hallucination handling story**
- **One-product company** — vulnerable to "AI SDR" category compression

## Implications for 10XAI — adopt these patterns wholesale
1. **Publish at least one transparent-pricing tier.** $900/mo is AiSDR's most under-rated trust signal. 10XAI should expose at least a "Starter Operating System" public price.
2. **Build a free conversational tool that becomes the lead magnet.** AiSDR's "AI Strategist" (20 min, 5 campaign ideas from a URL) is THE most replicable feature. The 10XAI equivalent: drop in your business URL → get a personalized "AI Operations Map" — the 6 systems prioritized for *your* business, with $ value estimates. This is also a perfect demo of what 10XAI does.
3. **Build a comparisons section.** Direct comparison pages capture commercial-intent search traffic. 10XAI should have "/vs-11x", "/vs-artisan", "/vs-aisdr", "/vs-relevance-ai", "/vs-take-blip" pages each in EN/PT-BR/ES.
4. **The "stack consolidation" pitch is the strongest economic story.** 10XAI should price by *tools replaced* not hours saved.
5. **Annual industry report = lead magnet gold.** "State of AI for SMBs in LatAm 2026" — gated, distributed via founder LinkedIn. Position Bernardo as the source.
6. **Vertical SEO pages.** AiSDR has "for healthcare," "for SaaS," etc. 10XAI should ship "for dental practices," "for accounting firms," "for marketing agencies" — each with case study + ICP-specific copy.
