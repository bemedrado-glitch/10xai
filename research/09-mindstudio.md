## Implications for 10XAI

### Strategic build-vs-buy decision
MindStudio (or similar — Lindy.ai builder, n8n, Make, Stack AI) could be **the engine 10XAI builds on**, not competes with. This is the single biggest stack decision. Two paths:

1. **10XAI as Solutions Partner:** Build agents on MindStudio (or alternate). Sell to SMBs. Faster to market, lower margin, vendor lock-in risk.
2. **10XAI builds its own thin platform:** Custom infra (Vercel + Supabase + direct LLM calls). Higher margin, slower, more diff'd.

Phase 2 strategy report must answer this. **Recommended hybrid:** ship the marketing site + lead-gen tools on custom infra; deliver client agents on a chosen agent platform (likely MindStudio or n8n + Claude). Best of both.

### Other implications
1. **The bootcamp/community model is legit.** 10XAI should run a "AI Operations Bootcamp for SMBs in LatAm" — free, recorded, drives top-of-funnel.
2. **Comparison-page SEO is a moat.** MindStudio's comparison posts to incumbents capture commercial-intent traffic. 10XAI should build "/vs-take-blip" / "/vs-zapier-ai" / "/vs-make-com" / "/vs-relevance" pages in three languages.
3. **The agency partner program is the long game.** 10XAI eventually offers white-label of its own engine to other LatAm agencies and consultancies. Structure for this from day one (multi-tenant data model + branded URLs).
