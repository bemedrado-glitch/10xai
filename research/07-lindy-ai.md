## Implications for 10XAI
1. **The creator-influencer GTM is wide open in PT-BR / ES.** No equivalent of Lenny / Greg Isenberg coverage exists for AI tools in LatAm tech Twitter/LinkedIn. Bernardo's media plan should target equivalent voices: Brazilian operators, Mexican founders, Argentine SaaS leaders.
2. **Transparent SMB pricing converts.** $49–60/mo is a frictionless self-serve price. 10XAI's "Starter" tier should sit in this same band ($49–99/mo), or come close.
3. **Privacy/compliance copy isn't optional.** Even SMBs scan for it. The 10XAI site needs a public Trust page from day one.
4. **Personal AI ≠ business AI.** Lindy wins individuals. 10XAI's whitespace is *team operating systems* — a deliberate altitude shift away from Lindy.
