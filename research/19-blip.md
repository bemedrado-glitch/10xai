## ★ Strategic posture for 10XAI

Blip cannot be ignored. Three plausible positions:

### Position A — **Underbid by ICP** (Recommended)
- Blip = enterprise (Itaú, Coca-Cola tier)
- 10XAI = SMB (15–500 employees) — too small for Blip's sales team
- Don't sell against Blip; sell *under* it. Blip is the platform; 10XAI is the agency for the SMBs Blip can't profitably serve.

### Position B — **Become a Blip Partner**
- Apply to Blip Partners program
- Use Blip as WhatsApp infra for client engagements
- Trade some margin for go-to-market trust + WhatsApp BSP access (which is otherwise hard to obtain)
- Possible long-term path. Worth investigating for the WhatsApp piece specifically.

### Position C — **Build adjacent infrastructure**
- Use Twilio / Meta direct for WhatsApp instead of Blip
- Compete on price + speed + SMB-specific UX
- Risk: WhatsApp BSP access takes time
- More differentiation, more cost

★ **Recommended:** **Hybrid A + B.** Position 10XAI as SMB-tier (under Blip's enterprise market) AND apply to Blip Partners to leverage WhatsApp BSP infrastructure for client deliveries. Use Blip's existing channel for WhatsApp; use 10XAI's own infra for everything else.
