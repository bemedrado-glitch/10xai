## Weaknesses (gaps 10XAI can exploit)
- **Zero pricing transparency** → SMBs self-disqualify
- **Sales-gated everything** → no PLG, no free trial, no self-serve
- **Enterprise focus** → leaves the SMB segment (15–500 employees) wide open
- **English-centric** → multilingual capability claimed but not productized for ES/PT-BR markets
- **No agency / partner play** — pure direct sales

## Implications for 10XAI
1. **Compete on accessibility, not power.** SMBs can't get past 11x's demo wall. 10XAI can win with public pricing and a self-serve "Starter" tier.
2. **Localize hard.** 11x is an English-speaking US+UK product. Real PT-BR / ES copy + native-speaker AI is a moat in LatAm.
3. **Don't try to out-fund them on outbound AI.** Position 10XAI as the *full operating system* (sales + LMS + customer service + RFP + ops) — not just an SDR agent.
4. **Adopt their two-agent narrative pattern** (outbound + inbound) — it's clear and clean. Adapt to "AI SDR + AI CS rep" or similar paired story.
