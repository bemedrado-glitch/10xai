## Gaps the new build must close

| # | Gap | Why it matters | Fix in v2 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Zero founder visibility** | SMBs buying AI from a small agency need a real human. Salesforce + 20-yr healthcare/sales credibility is hidden. | Founder-front layout. Bernardo's face, story, certifications, LinkedIn. |
| 2 | **No verified case studies** | Site uses "aspirational" public examples with disclaimer. Trust killer. | At least 1–2 real case studies before launch (even pilot/free engagements). |
| 3 | **Pricing opacity** | ROI calc but no packages. Buyers self-disqualify or stall. | At least one "Starter" public package + transparent ranges for the rest. |
| 4 | **No real ES support** | Implied via LatAm framing only. | True trilingual (EN/PT-BR/ES) routing from day one. |
| 5 | **Generic/duplicate imagery** | Same hero graphic repeated. Low trust signal. | Custom illustrations, real product screenshots, founder photos. |
| 6 | **No content engine** | No blog, no guides, no SEO surface. | Content map per locale + at least 6 cornerstone articles by launch. |
| 7 | **Vague methodology** | "2–4 weeks" stated; the *how* is hidden. | Public methodology page (the 10XAI delivery system). |
| 8 | **No embedded tools** | Site is brochure-only. No reason for a prospect to interact. | Embedded mini-tools (lead finder, ROI calc that *outputs* a real plan, etc.) — these become both qualifier and SEO magnet. |

## What this means for the rebuild

The new site/app isn't a redesign — it's a **trust engine + lead engine** built around a real founder, real proof, real packages, real tools. Aesthetics matter, but they're table stakes; the moat is execution credibility + interactive tooling.
