## Implications for 10XAI
1. **"Operates like your team" maps perfectly to "Operating System for SMBs."** Adopt the framing — the difference is altitude (Cassidy = enterprise context platform; 10XAI = SMB outcome-as-a-service).
2. **The accessibility hero story (editor → AI builder) is gold.** 10XAI should publish a parallel: a Brazilian dental practice owner / a Texas accounting firm partner / a Mexican logistics operator who built their AI ops without code. *Stories of operators, not stories of code.*
3. **Run a partner program from day one.** Cassidy validates that even mid-stage AI platforms benefit from referral mechanics. 10XAI's "no-website flywheel" naturally enables this — local marketing agencies can resell the whole engine.
4. **"Document Verification" / accuracy-over-time copy is differentiating.** Buyers worry agents drift. 10XAI should publish how it handles this (regular human audits, golden datasets, sample-based QA).
