## Implications for 10XAI
1. **Jasper proves the "100 specialized agents" architecture.** 10XAI's "6 operating systems" is similar but tighter. Stay tight: 6 well-named systems beats 100 generic ones for SMB cognitive load.
2. **Brand governance is THE enterprise unlock.** SMBs care less, but 10XAI's mid-market (15–500 employees) tier should have a "Brand Voice for Your AI" feature — agents trained on your tone-of-voice docs. Differentiates vs. Hocoos / Durable cookie-cutter copy.
3. **The certification program is leverage.** 10XAI could run a free certification: "10XAI Operator Certified — for SMB founders who want to install AI ops themselves." Trains future buyers AND creates a community pool.
4. **Jasper's PT-BR / ES gap = your beachhead.** Enterprise buyers in LatAm are increasingly priced out of Jasper because there's no localized voice training. 10XAI could land mid-market LatAm enterprises that Jasper can't serve.
