## Implications for 10XAI
1. **G2 + review-site SEO is real.** Regie's badges are a moat. 10XAI should prioritize getting ON G2 / Capterra / SourceForge LatAm equivalents (B2B Stack in Brazil) early.
2. **"Force multiplier" framing > "Replace your team."** Sales leaders fear AI as a layoff tool. The 10XAI positioning needs to lead with augmentation language for buyers, even if the math is cost-replacement.
3. **Persona pages convert.** Regie has separate copy for SDR / AE / Sales Leader / RevOps / Marketing. 10XAI's six "systems" should each have an audience-led twin (e.g., "for dental practice owners," "for accounting firm partners," "for marketing agency founders").
4. **Build a real ROI calculator.** Regie's calc + the existing 10xai.us calc are baseline. The new site should output a *personalized 90-day plan PDF* — converting the calc from a curiosity into a lead magnet.
